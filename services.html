Clinical Practice

Psychotherapy & Counseling Services

Evidence-based, culturally sensitive psychotherapy delivered with compassion — in Zanzibar and online for clients worldwide.

Individual Therapy

Personalised one-on-one psychotherapy tailored to your unique needs, delivered in a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental space.

  • Anxiety & depression counseling
  • Grief & loss support
  • Life transitions & personal growth
  • Trauma & PTSD therapy (CBT, TF-CBT)
  • Stress management & burnout recovery
  • GBV survivor psychosocial support
  • Cross-cultural & identity counseling

Group Therapy

Structured therapeutic group sessions that foster shared healing, peer support, and community resilience in a guided environment.

  • Psychoeducation & mental health awareness groups
  • Trauma recovery support groups
  • GBV survivor group sessions
  • Stigma reduction & community healing circles
  • Youth mental health groups
  • Stress & coping skills workshops

Family Therapy

Culturally sensitive family counseling that strengthens communication, resolves conflict, and restores healthy relational dynamics within families.

  • Family conflict resolution & mediation
  • Parent–child relationship counseling
  • Domestic violence recovery support
  • Grief & bereavement for families
  • Parenting skills & child development guidance
  • Blended & cross-cultural family counseling

Online Therapy

Secure, confidential teletherapy for clients worldwide — bringing professional psychological support to your home, wherever you are.

  • Individual online counseling sessions
  • Virtual couples & family therapy
  • Online group therapy & workshops
  • Mental health support for the Tanzanian diaspora
  • Cross-cultural & international student counseling
  • Flexible scheduling across time zones

Organisations & NGOs

Institutional & NGO Services

Partnering with governments, NGOs, INGOs, and academic institutions to build sustainable, culturally grounded mental health systems.

Consulting & Advisory

Strategic mental health advisory for ministries, NGOs, and health systems. Experienced working with USAID, Jhpiego, and the Zanzibar Ministry of Health.

Workshops & Training

Capacity-building workshops in MHPSS, trauma-informed practice, CBT, counseling ethics, and staff resilience for frontline health workers, social workers, and educators.

Psychosocial Programme Design

End-to-end design and implementation of community mental health programmes, GBV response systems, and integrated psychosocial support services — evidence-based and locally contextualised.

MHPSS for NGOs & INGOs

Mental Health and Psychosocial Support integration into humanitarian and development programmes. Specialised in African contexts, stigma reduction, and cross-cultural approaches.
