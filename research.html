Research & Academic Profile

Research Interests

  • Psychopathology & mental health integration in low-resource settings
  • Stigma reduction and health-seeking behaviour
  • Cultural and social factors in psychological wellbeing
  • Gender-based violence and psychosocial support systems
  • Community mental health in East Africa
  • CBT adaptations for African cultural contexts

Teaching & Supervision

Taught 250+ students at Zanzibar University and Zanzibar School of Health. Supervised 65+ students in research and clinical fieldwork. Contributed to curriculum development in CBT, counseling ethics, and neurodevelopmental disorders.

Google Scholar ResearchGate ORCID

Selected Publications & Contributions

Social Anxiety Among University Students in East Africa

Peer-reviewed research examining the prevalence and cultural predictors of social anxiety in East African university students.

Published · Peer-Reviewed Journal

Vaccine Uptake Initiative — Zanzibar (+45% Increase)

Led multisectoral public health collaboration with USAID, Jhpiego, and the Zanzibar Ministry of Health, achieving a documented 45% increase in community vaccine uptake.

Public Health Contribution · USAID / Jhpiego Partnership

Jitima Multidisciplinary Centre — Programme Development

Co-designed and established an integrated psychosocial support centre serving 350,000+ individuals, leading a 20+ person team of doctors, psychologists, social workers, police, and nurses.

Programme Design · Zanzibar Ministry of Health
